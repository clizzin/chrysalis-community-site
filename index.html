Chrysalis Community

Chrysalis is an intentional community house in Berkeley, California, on the ancestral lands of the Chochenyo Ohlone people. In addition to living together, we try to hold space for our wider community, share resources, and organize collective action.

Projects

Ongoing
  • Black Lives Matter Donation Matching: matching up to $10,000 in donations to Bay Area organizations working for racial justice across a variety of issues.
Past
  • COVID-19 Community Support Fund: zero-interest loans for people facing financial difficulties because of COVID-19. This project has closed, but we're keeping up links to our public documents so you can use them as templates for any lending efforts you would like to organize.
